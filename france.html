DAY 2: Eiffel Tower.

The Eiffel Tower is a wrought-iron lattice tower on the Champ de Mars in Paris, France. It is named after the engineer Gustave Eiffel, whose company designed and built the tower.

Facts you should know
  • Neighbourhood: The 7th Arrondissement
  • Weather: this time of year,the temperature rages between 13 and 30 deg celcius

  4. An optional museum tour after the meal.
  5. A drive back to te hotel.
  6. Access to a personal vehicle to explore the area.
