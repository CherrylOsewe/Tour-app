Egypt In 3 Days

DAY 1: Pyramids of Giza.

The pyramids of Giza were royal tombs built for three different pharaohs. The northernmost and oldest pyramid of the group was built for Khufu (Greek: Cheops), the second king of the 4th dynasty. Called the Great Pyramid, it is the largest of the three.

Facts you should know
  • Neighbourhood: Al Giza Dessert
  • Weather- this time of year,the temperature rages between 26 and 39 deg celcius

  2. Hotel check in.

  4. A drive to the tombs.
  5. A tour through the tombs with lunch there after.
  6. A free afternoon for the guests and dinner at a local restaurant.

DAY 2: Valley of Kings.

One of the most famous archaeological sites in the world, this is a royal burial ground, with exquisitely decorated tombs for the pharaohs who ruled Egypt between 1539 and 1075 BC. There are over 60 tombs in this small area,

DAY 3: Giftun Islands.

The Giftun Islands are two islands in the Red Sea near Hurghada in Egypt. Giftun Kebir or Big Giftun is located further west and closer to Hurghada. Giftun Soraya or Little Giftun is further east.
