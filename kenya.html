Kenya In 3 Days

DAY 1: Nairobi National Park.

Located in the heart of Nairobi city,this is the only national park contained in a capital city

Facts you should know
  • County its located in -Nairobi,047
  • Weather- this time of year,the temperature rages between 19 and 21 deg celcius
    Our day one package covers:
  1. Your evening flight into Nairobi the previous day.
  2. Hotel check in.
  3. Bed and breakfast.
  4. A day tour and lunch at the park.
  5. A night drive through Nairobi streets.

DAY 2:Diani Beach.

The pearl white shores of Diani beach lie on the coast line of Kenya in Kwale

Facts you should know
  • County its located in -Kwale ,002
  • Weather- this time of year,the temperature rages between 22 and 28 deg celcius

  3. Hotel check in.
  4. Lunch at the hotel followed by a stroll on the beach.
  5. Access to a personal vehicle to explore the area.
